Owner: Operations Tower Leadership
Status: Approved

Applies to: Operations Tower Employees

## 1. What Operations Does

Operations covers business process outsourcing (BPO) style delivery, transaction processing, customer support operations, back-office processing, and process improvement (Lean/Six Sigma) engagements for client business functions.

## 2. Common Technologies Used

1. Process platforms: client-specific case management and workflow tools.
2. Robotic Process Automation (RPA): UiPath, Automation Anywhere, Blue Prism, used to automate repetitive transaction processing.
3. Reporting/quality: client-specific dashboards, Capgemini's internal quality and SLA tracking tools.

## 3. Mandatory Tower Trainings

See Mandatory Trainings document (TRN-MAND-009), Operations section: Process Compliance and SLA Management (required within 14 days) and Client Data Handling for Operations Roles (required within 14 days) — this last one is especially strict given the volume of personally identifiable information (PII) often processed in Operations roles.

## 4. Ways of Working

1. Operations roles are frequently measured against strict SLA metrics: Average Handle Time (AHT), accuracy rate, and Turnaround Time (TAT), reviewed weekly by your Team Lead.
2. Shift-based work is common, particularly for client engagements spanning different time zones.
3. Quality audits are conducted regularly (frequency varies by project) by a dedicated Quality Analyst team, separate from your reporting manager.

## 5. Who to Contact

1. SLA/performance metric questions: your Team Lead or Operations Manager.
2. Tower allocation/staffing questions: Operations Resource Manager.
3. Process documentation or Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) access: your project's Process Excellence team.
