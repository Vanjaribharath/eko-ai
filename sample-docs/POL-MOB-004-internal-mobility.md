# Internal Mobility and Internal Job Posting (IJP) Policy

Document ID: POL-MOB-004
Owner: HR Talent Management
Status: Approved
Version: 2.6
Last Updated: 2026-02-22
Applies to: All Employees, All Towers

## 1. Eligibility

1. Minimum tenure of 12 months in your current project/role is generally required before applying to an Internal Job Posting (IJP), though business-critical exceptions can be approved by both current and target managers.
2. Employees must not be on an active Performance Improvement Plan (PIP) to apply for internal mobility.

## 2. How to Apply

1. Browse open positions on the Internal Job Portal, accessible from the HR Portal homepage.
2. Apply directly through the portal; your current manager is notified automatically once you reach the interview stage, not at the point of application, to protect confidentiality during early exploration.
3. Interview process for internal moves is typically lighter than external hiring but still includes a technical/functional round and a culture/fit conversation with the target team.

## 3. Tower Changes

1. Moving between towers (for example, ITS to Digital, or Operations to EIS) is permitted through the same IJP process; some tower changes may require additional upskilling agreed upon before the transition.
2. See the New to Project Guide (ONB-NTP-006) for what changes and what stays the same when you move, including tower realignment timelines in HR systems.

## 4. Compensation on Internal Move

1. Internal moves typically do not include a compensation change unless the move includes a grade promotion, in which case standard promotion compensation guidelines apply.
2. Relocation support, if the new role requires a different base location, follows the Reimbursement section of the Payroll Policy (POL-PAY-014), Section 4.

## 5. Cooling-Off Period

After completing an internal move, a cooling-off period of typically 6-12 months applies before you can apply for another internal move, except in cases of project closure or other business-driven reallocation outside your control.
