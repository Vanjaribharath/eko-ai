# Terms and Conditions of Employment

Document ID: POL-TNC-001
Owner: Legal / HR Operations
Status: Approved
Version: 9.0
Last Updated: 2026-01-08
Applies to: All Employees, All Towers

## 1. Nature of Employment

1. Employment with Capgemini is governed by the appointment letter issued at joining, this Terms and Conditions document, and applicable local labor law.
2. Probation period is typically 6 months from date of joining, during which either party may terminate employment with a shorter notice period as specified in the appointment letter.

## 2. Confidentiality and Intellectual Property

1. All work product, code, designs, and documentation created during employment, on company time or using company/client resources, belongs to Capgemini or the relevant client, not the employee.
2. Confidentiality obligations under this agreement survive termination of employment indefinitely for client-confidential information, and for a minimum of 3 years for general company-confidential information.
3. Never share, copy, or retain client project materials, source code, or documents after offboarding, regardless of personal involvement in creating them.

## 3. Non-Solicitation and Non-Compete

1. For 12 months after leaving Capgemini, employees agree not to solicit current Capgemini employees to join a competing organization.
2. Specific non-compete clauses, if applicable to your role or grade, are detailed in your individual appointment letter — not all roles carry this clause.

## 4. Code of Conduct Reference

This document works alongside the Code of Conduct and Confidentiality Policy (POL-COC-001); in case of any conflict between general terms and the specific confidentiality rules (such as the prohibition on photographing client material or using unapproved AI tools), the more specific and restrictive policy applies.

## 5. Termination

1. Voluntary resignation requires notice as per your appointment letter (commonly 60-90 days depending on grade).
2. Capgemini may terminate employment for cause (policy violation, performance, misconduct) following due process as outlined in the Disciplinary Policy.
3. Final settlement (F&F) is processed per the Payroll Policy (POL-PAY-014), Section 6.

## 6. Amendments

Capgemini reserves the right to amend these terms with reasonable notice, communicated through official HR channels (HR Portal announcements, email). Continued employment after such notice constitutes acceptance of amended terms.

## 7. Governing Law

These terms are governed by the labor laws of the country/state in which the employee is employed, as specified in the appointment letter.
