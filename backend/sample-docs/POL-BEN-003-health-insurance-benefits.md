# Health Insurance and Employee Benefits

Document ID: POL-BEN-003
Owner: HR Benefits Team
Status: Approved
Version: 4.2
Last Updated: 2026-01-28
Applies to: All Employees, All Towers

## 1. Group Medical Insurance (GMI)

1. All employees are covered under Group Medical Insurance from date of joining, with coverage typically extending to spouse and up to 2 children; parents coverage may be available as an optional add-on at additional cost.
2. E-cards are issued within 15-20 working days of joining; physical cards follow shortly after, accessible via HR Portal > Benefits > Insurance.
3. Cashless treatment is available at network hospitals (list available on the insurance provider's portal); reimbursement claims apply for non-network hospital treatment.

## 2. Optional Benefits

1. Voluntary additional insurance top-up plans are available during the annual enrollment window, typically in April.
2. Meal cards/vouchers, if applicable to your grade, are part of the flexible benefits structure within your CTC — configurable during the annual tax declaration window.
3. National Pension Scheme (NPS) employer contribution is available as an optional CTC restructuring component.

## 3. Wellness Programs

1. Employee Assistance Program (EAP) provides confidential counseling support (mental health, financial, legal guidance) accessible 24x7 via a dedicated helpline, free for all employees and immediate family.
2. Periodic health check-up camps and wellness webinars are organized at major locations; schedules published on HR Portal > Wellness.

## 4. Claims Process

1. Cashless: present your e-card at a network hospital; pre-authorization may be required for planned (non-emergency) procedures.
2. Reimbursement: submit original bills and discharge summary through the insurance provider's portal within the claim window specified by the provider (commonly 30 days from discharge).
3. Claim rejections or delays beyond the provider's stated turnaround should be escalated through HR Portal > Benefits > Insurance Helpdesk.

## 5. Common Questions

1. Adding a new dependent (marriage, childbirth) is done through HR Portal > Benefits > Add Dependent, ideally within 30 days of the life event to avoid coverage gaps.
2. Coverage during notice period continues until your last working day; post-exit continuation options (if any) are communicated during offboarding.
