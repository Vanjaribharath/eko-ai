# Background Verification (BGV) Policy

Document ID: POL-BGV-002
Owner: HR Talent Acquisition
Status: Approved
Version: 2.9
Last Updated: 2026-01-05
Applies to: All New Joiners, All Towers

## 1. Purpose and Scope

Background verification is conducted for all new joiners prior to or shortly after joining, as required by company policy and many client contracts which mandate verified staffing.

## 2. What Is Verified

1. Identity verification (government ID proof).
2. Educational qualification verification (degree certificates, transcripts).
3. Employment history verification (previous employers, typically last 2-3 organizations or 7 years, whichever is shorter).
4. Address verification (current and permanent address).
5. Criminal record check, where legally permissible in the employee's jurisdiction.
6. Reference checks, typically 1-2 professional references from prior employment.

## 3. Process and Timeline

1. BGV is initiated through a third-party verification partner once the offer is accepted; the candidate receives a verification portal link to submit documents.
2. Standard turnaround is 7-15 working days, though delays can occur due to slow employer responses for previous-employment verification.
3. Joining can proceed in parallel with BGV in most cases ("BGV in progress" status), but unresolved discrepancies found later can affect continued employment depending on severity.

## 4. Discrepancies

1. Minor discrepancies (e.g., address formatting differences) are usually resolved with clarification from the candidate.
2. Significant discrepancies (falsified education, undisclosed employment gaps misrepresented, criminal record non-disclosure) are escalated to HR and may result in offer withdrawal or termination, even after joining.
3. If you are contacted by the verification partner with a discrepancy notice, respond promptly with clarifying documents — delays in response are often mistaken for non-cooperation.

## 5. Client-Specific Additional BGV

1. Some clients (particularly in BFSI, healthcare, and government-adjacent Operations engagements) require additional client-specific BGV beyond the standard Capgemini process before project access is granted.
2. This additional check is coordinated by your Resource Manager and Project Manager once project allocation is confirmed, and may take an additional 1-3 weeks.

## 6. Questions

Direct BGV-specific questions to the Talent Acquisition BGV helpdesk, accessible via the joining portal link sent during your offer process, or HR Portal > Onboarding > BGV Status for existing joiners checking progress.
