## 1. What ITS Does

ITS (IT Services) covers infrastructure management, service desk operations, network administration, cloud infrastructure support, and IT service management (ITSM) for client environments — keeping client IT systems running, monitored, and compliant.

## 2. Common Technologies Used

1. ITSM platforms: ServiceNow, BMC Remedy.
2. Infrastructure: VMware, Windows Server, Linux administration, AWS/Azure infrastructure services.
3. Monitoring: Splunk, Dynatrace, Nagios, client-specific monitoring stacks.
4. Frameworks: ITIL v4 (Incident, Problem, Change, and Service Level Management).

## 3. Mandatory Tower Trainings

See Mandatory Trainings document (TRN-MAND-009), ITS section: ITIL Foundations (required within 30 days for service management roles) and Infrastructure Security Basics (required within 14 days).

## 4. Ways of Working

1. ITS commonly operates with shift-based coverage (including 24x7 rotations for production support engagements) — shift schedules are managed by your Shift Lead.
2. Incident response follows strict Service Level Agreements (SLAs); Severity 1 incidents require immediate escalation per the client's escalation matrix.
3. Change management for infrastructure changes requires CAB (Change Advisory Board) approval for anything touching production systems.

## 5. Who to Contact

1. Shift schedule or rotation questions: your Shift Lead or Delivery Manager.
2. Tower allocation/staffing questions: ITS Resource Manager.
3. On-call compensation or night-shift allowance questions: HRBP, citing the specific shift policy applicable to your project.
