## 1. What AuthEnq Does

AuthEnq (Authentication and Enquiry Systems) handles identity, authentication, single sign-on (SSO), access management, and enquiry-handling systems for client organizations. Typical engagements include implementing and supporting Identity and Access Management (IAM) platforms, SSO integrations, multi-factor authentication rollouts, and customer/employee enquiry portals tied to identity verification.

## 2. Common Technologies Used

1. Identity providers: Okta, Ping Identity, Azure AD / Entra ID, ForgeRock.
2. Protocols: SAML, OAuth 2.0, OpenID Connect (OIDC).
3. Directory services: LDAP, Active Directory.
4. Enquiry/case management systems vary by client, commonly ServiceNow or client-proprietary platforms.

## 3. Mandatory Tower Trainings

See Mandatory Trainings document (TRN-MAND-009), AuthEnq section: Authentication Systems Security Fundamentals and Identity and Access Management (IAM) Basics, both required within 14 days of allocation.

## 4. Ways of Working

1. Most AuthEnq projects run on 2-week Agile sprints with daily stand-ups.
2. Change requests touching production authentication systems require a formal Change Advisory Board (CAB) approval due to the sensitivity of identity infrastructure.
3. Access to client identity systems is provisioned on a strict need-to-know basis, often requiring additional client-side background checks beyond standard Capgemini BGV.

## 5. Who to Contact

1. Tower-specific technical questions: your Tech Lead or Solution Architect.
2. Tower allocation/staffing questions: AuthEnq Resource Manager.
3. Tower-specific training access issues: contact your HRBP, citing "AuthEnq" explicitly.
